Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Robotics and Computer Vision Lab at KAIST, South Korea, under the supervision of Prof. Kweon In So. My research focuses on deep learning with a focus on robustness and security. Prior to the PhD course, I worked at LS Electric for 2 and half years. I got two two master degrees: Engineering & policy Analysis at TU Delft and Electrical Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology.
